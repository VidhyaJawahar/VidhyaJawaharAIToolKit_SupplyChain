Literature Mapping Summary

The selected literature provides a comprehensive overview of Sustainable Supply Chain Management (SSCM)...

Key works include Stindt’s (2017) integrated planning model, Ansari and Kant’s (2017) review and synthesis, and contributions by Dubey, Khan, and Rajeev, guiding future SSCM research.

Studies like Mastos and Gotzamani (2022), and Mathivathanan et al. (2018), apply SSCM in industries like food and automotive. Technologies such as blockchain and e-business are rising, while challenges in developing economies demand strategic adoption.

Future research should explore integration, performance tools, and cross-sector applications to scale SSCM globally.
